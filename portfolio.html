Hyperrealism

Hello there. My art style of choice is hyperrealism. I've been drawing off and on, pretty much my entire life, but just recently got into hyperrealism a bit after the Covid pandemic began. I spent a lot of time watching other artists on youtube till I eventually stumbled down the rabbit hole that got me here today. The patience it takes to dedicate the time and effort into this style can be rough, but also very therapeutic.
