Some Resources to Learn More

How to Draw Hyperrealistic Lips - Jono Dry

A step by step tutorial by Jono Dry on how to draw hyper-realistic lips from scratch.

Drawing tools I CAN'T live without - Jono Dry

Another video made by Professional Hyperrealism Artist, Jono Dry, walking you through the many different tools that are very useful for getting those photo-realistic drawings.

Hyperrealism Drawing Tutorial Workshop Preview - Dirk Dzimirsky

Here's a video by Dirk Dzimirsky, showing various techniques for achieving that realism for hyper-realistic drawings, specializing in charcoal for those interested.

HYPERREALISM - Emanuele Dascanio

Here's an amazing time-lapse video, made by Emanuele Dascanio, of a drawing that took a little over 300 hours. The mediums used here are both charcoal AND graphite.

"FIERCE" - Time-lapse - ZokArt

A Time-lapse drawing by ZokArt that took about 430 hours. He also uses charcoal and graphite to really get those tones that make the drawing pop.

SKIN PORES DRAWING TUTORIAL - paw annath Arts

Here's another good video to learn some more hyperrealism technique, featureing a graphite artist who shows some good ways to produce skin tones and pores for realism.
